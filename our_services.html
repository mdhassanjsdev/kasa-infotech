1. IT Staffing

We specialize in providing staffing services to our clients. This can be a contract, contract-to-hire, and permanent placement. Our large network of consultants helps us in finding resources that are not actively looking and hence are not on job boards. Our goal is to be a partner to our client and not just a staffing provider.

At Kasa InfoTech, recruiting process management is flexible and customized for each client to meet their specific needs. Our team can serve as an extension of your HR department, adding seasonal capacity, ongoing support, or areas of specialty. We can also take the entire recruiting process, from application performance management to background checks to administrative support. You choose exactly the talent management solutions you need, whether that means the ability to hire a new candidate to fill a high-skill position or a total redesign of your recruitment process.

Why to choose our service:

1 . We reduce the number of hours the client’s staff work on staff projects by providing excellent resources so that you do not have to interview multiple candidates to get the right resource.

2 . We make the onboarding and administrative process seamless and quick.

3 . We submit all suitable candidates to the client’s hiring manager for their final interview, but apart from that our hope is to add no additional tasks.

4 . We do not have any space or equipment requirements from our clients as all our tasks will be done from our offices.
